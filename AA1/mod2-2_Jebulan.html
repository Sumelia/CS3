SK8 The Infinity

Reki, a high school sophomore and skater, is addicted to "S", a highly secret skateboarding race that takes place in an abandoned mine. The skaters are especially wild about the heated battles that erupt in the races. One day, Reki invites Langa, a transfer student from Canada, to an "S" race. Langa has no skateboarding experience as he's a snowboarder but finds himself pulled into the crazy world of "S" Along the way they experience a ton of memories and emotions that come with growing and maturing. They also managed to meet close friends from "S".

My Top 5 Favourite Characters in SK8 The Infinity

  2. Miya Chinen
  3. Cherry Blossom

Exo

The discography of the South Korean-Chinese boy band Exo consists of seven studio albums, seven extended plays, four live albums and twenty-eight singles. The group's first release, the extended play Mama, was released in April 2012. The album topped Korean music charts on its release. With the release of Don't Mess Up My Tempo in November 2018, Exo became the first South Korean artist who debuted in the 21st century to sell 10 million albums cumulatively.

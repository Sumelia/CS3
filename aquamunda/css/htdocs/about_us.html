About Us

For the better of the waters!

This website aims to help and share knowledge about an occuring social problem that may have dire effects on us and our world when we continue to not do anything. This social issue is 'Water Pollution'. There are a lot of unfortunate things that may happen when Water Pollution is not known and not taken careful measures against. Therefore, this website is here to hopefully provide the lots with information vital for the cleaniness of our waters. Thank you!

*Most of the information are from : https://www.nrdc.org/stories/water-pollution-everything-you-need-know and https://www.simsbury-ct.gov/water-pollution-control/pages/ten-things-you-can-do-to-reduce-water-pollution.*
